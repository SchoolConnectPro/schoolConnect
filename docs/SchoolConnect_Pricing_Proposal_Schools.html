1. What is SchoolConnect?

SchoolConnect is a complete school communication and management platform that connects teachers, parents, and school administration — all through WhatsApp, the app every parent already uses.

No new app to download. No login to remember. Parents receive everything directly on WhatsApp.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if we send more messages than our monthly quota?
You will receive an alert on WhatsApp when you reach 80% of your quota. If you reach 100%, messages will be queued and you can either purchase a top-up pack or upgrade your plan. Emergency alerts are never blocked regardless of quota.
Q: Do parents need to download any app?
No. Parents receive all communications directly on WhatsApp — the app they already use every day. There is nothing new to install or learn.
Q: Is our school's data safe and private?
Yes. All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. Student and parent data is never shared with third parties. We comply with applicable data protection standards.
Q: Can we cancel at any time?
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. Annual plans can be cancelled with a pro-rated refund for unused months.
Q: How long does it take to set up?
Most schools are fully set up and live within 3–5 working days. Our team handles the technical setup; your staff only needs to attend a 1-hour training session.
Q: What if we have multiple branches or campuses?
Multi-campus support is available on the Premium plan. Each campus can be managed independently or together from a single dashboard.
Q: Can teachers use it without technical knowledge?
Absolutely. The dashboard is designed to be as simple as sending a WhatsApp message. No technical training is required for day-to-day use.
Q: Is there a free trial?
Yes. We offer a 30-day free trial with full access to the Growth plan features. No credit card required to start.
