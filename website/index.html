How It Works

From teacher's phone to every parent in seconds.

Three simple steps. Zero new behaviour for teachers. Maximum impact for parents.

01

Teacher Sends a WhatsApp

The teacher types a natural message on their own phone — exactly like texting a colleague. No special format. No new app. Just WhatsApp.

"Rahul absent today, grade 7A"
02

AI Formats & Previews

The AI understands the message, formats it professionally, and shows the teacher a preview. One tap to confirm — AI handles the rest.

📋 Preview ready for Rahul's parent. Reply SEND to notify.
03

Parents Receive Instantly

Parents receive a personalised WhatsApp notification in their preferred language — English, Hindi, or Punjabi. No app to install.
