1. How the Cost Structure Works

Every rupee a school pays you passes through multiple cost layers before becoming profit.

The 4 Cost Layers:

1. WhatsApp / Meta — Per-message charges (biggest variable cost)

2. BSP (Interakt) — Monthly platform subscription + per-message markup (bundled in Interakt prices)

3. LLM (AI) — Cost per AI query from parents (GPT-4o-mini / Gemini Flash)

4. Infrastructure — Railway (server), Neon (PostgreSQL), Cloudflare R2 (storage), Resend (email), Upstash Redis (cache), Vercel (frontend)

⚠ CRITICAL PRICING MODEL CHANGE (Nov 2024): Meta switched from per-conversation (24-hour window) billing to per-message billing for template messages. Service messages (parent-initiated inbound) are now FREE. All cost calculations in this document reflect the new per-message model.

2.2 Message Batching — Your Biggest Cost Lever

✅ Send one daily digest at ~3pm bundling homework + fee reminders + PTM info = 1 utility message charge regardless of how many items are in it.

✅ Attendance alerts (absent only) sent immediately in the morning as a separate message since timing matters to parents.

✅ Service messages are FREE — every parent AI query costs ₹0 in WhatsApp charges. Encourage parents to ask questions via WhatsApp.

5.2 PostgreSQL Database — Railway Built-in vs Neon

✅ Recommended: Use Railway built-in PostgreSQL. Railway lets you add a PostgreSQL service directly to your project — same dashboard, same billing, no separate account. It runs as a Docker container billed at Railway resource rates. For most SchoolConnect deployments this is simpler and sufficient.

📌 Decision guide:

Use Railway PostgreSQL if you want simplicity — one dashboard, one bill, no cold starts. Cost: ~$5-10/mo (~₹420-840/mo).

Use Neon Free tier if you want to save money in early stage (0-2 schools). Upgrade to Neon Launch when you outgrow the free tier.

For SchoolConnect at early stage: Railway PostgreSQL is the pragmatic choice. The $5-10/mo extra vs Neon free is worth the operational simplicity.

8. Cost Control Rules (Non-Negotiable)

⚠ These rules protect your margins. Violating them will make you unprofitable.

#RuleWhy It MattersImplementation
1Cap marketing broadcasts per planMarketing = ₹0.958/msg. 10 extra broadcasts = ₹4,790 extra cost for 500-student schoolStarter: 5 broadcasts/mo | Growth: 10/mo | Premium: 20/mo
2Always batch utility messages3 separate messages vs 1 batched = 3x costDaily digest at 3pm. Never send individual homework/fee alerts
3Rate limit AI queries10 queries/parent/day prevents abuseRedis rate limiter. Cache common answers
4Use GPT-4o-mini, not GPT-4oGPT-4o costs 16x more per queryOnly use GPT-4o for complex escalations (<5% of queries)
5Attendance alerts = absent onlySending present alerts doubles message volumeOnly trigger alert when student is marked absent
6Monitor monthly WhatsApp spendOne school sending 20 broadcasts = ₹9,580 in chargesSet up spend alerts in Interakt dashboard. Alert at 80% of expected
7Encourage parent-initiated queriesService messages are FREE. Push parents to ask questions vs. you sending updatesOnboarding message: "Message us anytime for updates"
